About Us

First established in 1948, The Attic began its life as a small bar above a greengrocers in Manchester, England. Hidden down a side street in the city center, The Attic was frequented by local tradesmen and politicians alike. Prefered by many for its moody interior and intimate corners, it was a place to get business done, make new acquiantances and socialise in private.

From its once humble beginnings, The Attic has been renovated into a much larger location. Now the number one Cocktail Lounge and Restaurant in Manchester City, we have expanded to new heights while maintaining the broody atomsphere that has made us a firm favourite throughout the years.
